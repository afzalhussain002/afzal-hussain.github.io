Research & Projects

Environmental Chemistry

My PhD project applies wastewater-based epidemiology to monitor public health by analysing pharmaceuticals as proxies for disease prevalence and burden. By interpreting spatio-temporal trends and socio-demographic influences, the work aims to provide evidence-based insights that support regulatory bodies in making informed decisions on healthcare planning, intervention strategies, and resource allocation.
